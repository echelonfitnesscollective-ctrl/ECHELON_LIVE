01ENTRY EXPERIENCE

FITNESS INTRO

Turn a first visit into a clear, confident next step.

  1. Before: Confirm reservation, coach, time, and what to bring.
  2. Deliver: Welcome, readiness conversation, baseline movement and goal review.
  3. Send: Same-day thank-you with their recommended Echelon path.
  4. Follow up: Create a 48-hour Coach Command task if no next step is booked.
02SIGNATURE COACHING

THE ECHELON 12

A 12-week coached strength and accountability experience.

  1. Accept: Review application and send acceptance, start date, expectations, and payment next step.
  2. Onboard: Confirm waiver, health intake, goals, photos, measurements, and training availability.
  3. Build: Publish Week 1, nutrition baseline, and the first check-in date in the Member Portal.
  4. Coach: Review weekly check-in, adjust the plan, reply to messages, and log notes.
  5. Finish: Complete Week 12 review, celebrate progress, and recommend renewal or 1-on-1 coaching.

03HIGH-TOUCH

1-ON-1 COACHING

Fully individual coaching with premium communication and accountability.

  1. Consult: Schedule a discovery call and document goal, schedule, limitations, and preferred communication.
  2. Confirm: Send the coaching agreement, schedule, payment plan, and portal invite.
  3. Program: Publish the first training block and explain how feedback and adjustments work.
  4. Retain: Hold a monthly progress review and set the next block before the current one ends.
04GROUP CLASS

FAITH & FAVOR MOBILITY

A restorative, welcoming class that keeps members moving and recovering well.

  1. Before class: Confirm roster, playlist, movement theme, and modification options.
  2. At check-in: Greet each member and flag anyone needing a scaled option.
  3. After class: Send a recovery reminder and invite the next reservation.
  4. Review: Track attendance weekly and personally follow up with first-time guests.
05GROUP CLASS

VL KINETIC

An athletic strength and conditioning format with a sharp, safe class flow.

  1. Before class: Build the stations, coach the intent, and prepare regressions before members arrive.
  2. At check-in: Confirm readiness and explain the day's performance focus.
  3. During class: Coach quality, effort, and safe progressions - not just speed.
  4. After class: Post the next class invitation and create a follow-up task for prospects.
