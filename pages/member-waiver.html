ASSUMPTION OF RISK & LIABILITY WAIVER

1. Voluntary participation and assumption of risk. I voluntarily choose to participate in Echelon Fitness Collective training, coaching, workouts, events, and related services. I understand that physical activity involves inherent risks, including falls, strains, sprains, illness, equipment failure, and serious injury. I knowingly accept these risks.

2. Health and medical responsibility. I confirm that I have provided accurate health information and will tell Echelon about any change in my health, injury, symptoms, medication, pregnancy/postpartum status, or other condition that could affect safe participation. I understand Echelon is not providing medical diagnosis or treatment, and I will seek medical advice or clearance when appropriate.

3. Training choices and conduct. I agree to follow coaching directions, use equipment responsibly, work within my personal limits, and stop activity if I feel pain, dizziness, chest discomfort, unusual shortness of breath, or another warning sign. I understand that results are not guaranteed.

4. Release of liability. To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, I release and hold harmless Echelon Fitness Collective and its owners, coaches, employees, contractors, and agents from claims arising from my voluntary participation, except where liability cannot legally be waived.

5. Emergency care. If an emergency occurs, I authorize Echelon personnel to seek emergency assistance for me when reasonably necessary. I remain responsible for related medical costs.

6. Electronic records and signature. I agree to receive and retain this agreement electronically. By checking the acknowledgments and typing my full legal name below, I intend to sign this agreement electronically and understand that Echelon will retain a record of my signature and signing date.

7. Severability and agreement. If any part of this agreement is unenforceable, the remaining portions remain in effect to the extent allowed by law. I have read, understood, and voluntarily agree to this document.
